About Our Team

Tom Bartlebaugh - Owner and Operator

Tom has been in the pest control industry for over 20 years. He started his career as a technician in a local company in Akron, Ohio, where he gained hands-on experience dealing with a wide variety of pests.

Over the years, Tom has attended numerous training sessions and workshops to stay updated with the latest pest control methods and technologies. His passion for helping people live pest-free lives led him to start Tom's Pest Control.

Tom is committed to providing trustworthy, efficient, and environmentally friendly services to the Akron community. When he's not exterminating pests, Tom enjoys spending time with his family and exploring the outdoors.

Trust Tom and his expertise to handle all your pest control needs.

With a background in biology and years of field experience, Tom ensures that every job is done right the first time.

He believes in continuous learning and often participates in industry conferences.

Tom's dedication to customer satisfaction has earned him a reputation as Akron's best pest control expert.
